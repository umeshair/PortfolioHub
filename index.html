Jagdish Ayer

Youth Leader & Activist

A passionate youth leader and activist dedicated to empowering young people to create positive change in their communities.

With over 15 years of dedicated leadership in Nepal's youth and civil society sectors, I have spearheaded impactful initiatives to empower young people, advance social justice, and strengthen democratic participation. My work spans capacity-building programs, advocacy campaigns, and grassroots recovery efforts, reaching thousands through trainings, lectures, and articles on critical themes like Human Rights, Leadership, SDGs, Gender Equality, SRHR, and Peacebuilding.
